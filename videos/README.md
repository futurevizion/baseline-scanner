# Videos

Place your own video clips here (`.mp4` recommended) and reference them in
`VIDEO_SEQUENCES` at the top of `src/main.py`.

No videos ship with this project. Film footage is copyrighted and cannot be
redistributed — create or source your own.

Suggested set:
- `welcome_1.mp4`, `welcome_2.mp4` — played when a known person is recognized
- `denied.mp4` — played for unknown faces
- `standby.mp4` — optional looping idle clip
